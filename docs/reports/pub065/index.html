Mass Deacidification: An Update on Possibilities and Limitations

pub65. Mass Deacidification: An Update on Possibilities and Limitations

date: Oct 1996

Porck, Henk (Oct 1996, 54pp.) ISBN: 1-887334-52-1.

Published jointly with the European Commission on Preservation and Access. A scientific review of five mass deacidification processes, including history, principles, research summary, and applications. Includes a critical evaluation of the process in general and extensive bibliography. Written for the nonspecialist.
