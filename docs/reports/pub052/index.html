The European Register of Microform Masters — Supporting International Cooperation

pub52. The European Register of Microform Masters — Supporting International Cooperation

date: May 1995

Schwartz, Werner (May 1995, 8pp.) ISBN: 1-887334-37-8.

Describes rationale for coordinated preservation effort in Europe and reviews EROMM’s growth from initial project phase to the international pilot database established in 1993. Briefly covers principles of establishing EROMM as a permanent service, including administration, installation as a database, access services, and systems support.
