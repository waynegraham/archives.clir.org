InÂ **1968**, CLR awards the San Antonio Public Library System $22,500 to explore the feasibility of books by mail as a regular feature of public library service in San Antonio and Bexar County, Texas. The experiment was a success; by 1970, San Antonio Public had permanently installed books-by-mail as a service to the user, and books by mail accounted for some 17% of total loans.
